A Brief History of UCR and the UC System

I was tasked by the Director of Talent Acquisition at UCR to create a video covering the history of the University of California and The University of California Riverside for their New Staff Orientation. Previously the NSO Facilitator used a 60 plus page slide deck, which would take about an hour to get through. The goal, cover the history of UC and UCR in under fifteen minutes. This documentary was created using Adobe Premier Pro, After Effects, Photoshop, and Audition. I provided the narration and used royalty free music from Adobe Stock music and Motion Array. My vision was to create a Ken Burns style documentary that was relevant, entertaining, and engaging to the viewer.

XBRL 101

The SEC requires that all financial documents be tagged to show relationships between linked numbers as well as explanations for values and categories. If documents are not tagged correctly, with XBRL, the SEC will reject the filing. After completing the XBRL series of courses the Customer Success Manager will be able to assist customers with their tagging needs.

Specifically, new analysts must complete this training prior to querying databases and submitting reports to requesting agencies.
